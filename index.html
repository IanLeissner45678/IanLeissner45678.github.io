22-Week Training Plan Overview

Your plan automatically progresses each week based on your race date (late July 2026). Current phase: Base Building

Phase 1: Base Building (Weeks 1-8)

Focus: Aerobic endurance, consistency, injury prevention

  • Build run volume from 15 to 25 miles/week
  • Build long ride from 50 to 70 miles
  • Maintain swim 8,000-10,000 yards/week
  • Weekly 90-minute brick sessions
  • Strength training 2x/week (Tuesday & Thursday)

Phase 2: Build (Weeks 9-16)

Focus: Increase intensity, race-specific work, volume peaks

  • Run builds to 30-35 miles/week, long runs to 12-13 miles
  • Long rides 75-90 miles, add tempo work
  • Add threshold sets in swim, practice race pace
  • Brick workouts increase to 2+ hours

Phase 3: Peak (Weeks 17-20)

Focus: Race simulation, maximum volume, confidence building

  • Run peaks at 35 miles/week, 13+ mile long runs
  • 90-100 mile rides, full race distance practice
  • Open water practice, race distance swims
  • Full race simulation with nutrition protocol

Phase 4: Taper (Weeks 21-22)

Focus: Reduce volume 40-60%, maintain intensity, fresh legs

  • Run 15-20 miles/week, short with some intensity
  • Bike 50-60 miles/week, keep legs sharp
  • Swim 5,000-6,000 yards/week
  • Short, snappy brick workouts
