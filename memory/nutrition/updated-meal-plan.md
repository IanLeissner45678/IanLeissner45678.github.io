# Updated Nutrition Plan - NO Nut Butters

## Dietary Preferences
⚠️ **PEANUT ALLERGY** - Avoid all peanuts
✅ **NO NUT BUTTERS** - No almond, sunflower seed, or cashew butter

## Preferred Alternatives
- **Avocado** - Healthy fats, great on toast or in meals
- **Hummus** - Protein and carbs, perfect for snacks
- **Cream cheese** - Protein and satisfying
- **Greek yogurt** - High protein (already in plan)
- **Honey/Jam** - Simple spreads for toast and bagels

## Race Day Nutrition Plan

### 3-4 Hours Before Race
**Breakfast** (200-300g carbs):
- Large bowl of oatmeal with banana, honey
- OR: Bagels with cream cheese and jam
- OR: Pancakes with maple syrup
- **Hydration**: 16-20 oz water with electrolytes

### During Race
**SWIM**: Nothing

**BIKE** (60-90g carbs/hour):
- Sports drink (20-24 oz/hour)
- 2-3 gels per hour
- Bananas + dates if available
- ⚠️ **Avoid aid station bars** - often contain nuts

**RUN** (30-60g carbs/hour):
- Aid station sports drink every stop
- 1-2 gels per hour
- ⚠️ **Skip aid station bars/trail mix** - nut contamination risk
